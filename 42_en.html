about my Piscine at 42.

Hello everyone,

I am Théo Guichard and I would like to share my journey at 42 with you. Everything starts with the famous Piscine: an intensive one-month immersion with no traditional classes or teachers, where learning comes from practice and peer-to-peer collaboration. During those four weeks, the pace is extreme: almost no weekends, very few breaks, and long days often ending late at night. The goal is to test endurance, motivation, and the ability to learn how to learn.

This unique experience shaped my rigor, time management under pressure, and teamwork skills. Behind each project are hours of debugging, collaboration, perseverance, and problem-solving. Every exercise is a piece of the puzzle that builds my 42 experience, in a school that values autonomy, creativity, and collaboration.

On my GitHub you will find a collection of these projects and exercises completed during and after the Piscine. I hope you will enjoy exploring them as much as I enjoyed creating them. Thank you for your interest.
