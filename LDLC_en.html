My journey at LDLC School

Hello everyone,

I am Théo Guichard, a student at LDLC School, an innovative school near Lyon that trains in digital technologies and entrepreneurship. This three-year program allows me to develop technical, managerial and human skills through a hands-on approach.

The curriculum is structured around three main areas: Digital Technologies & Innovation, Entrepreneurship & Management and Humanities & Perspectives. I learn not only software development tools but also team management, project leadership and business creation.

The training is based on the principle of learning by doing with internships, seminars and numerous concrete projects. It's a stimulating environment where we learn to work in teams, to innovate and to turn ideas into tangible achievements.

My goal is clear: to become a project manager in software development. I want to combine my programming skills with a global vision of project management to build useful and innovative solutions.

Each step of this journey brings me closer to that dream job, and I'm excited to share this adventure and my future projects with you.
